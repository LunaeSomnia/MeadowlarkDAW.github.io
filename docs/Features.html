Main features

Free & Open Source Forever

No paywalls, micro-transactions, free trials, locked features, or paying for pre-built binaries.

Parameter fiesta

Assign macros, LFOs, and envelopes to any parameter.

Meant to be Flexible

Easy FX chains, flexible grouping and sends anywhere! Organize your project however you like!

Wide Plugin Support

First-class support for the powerful and open-source CLAP plugin format. VST/VST3 plugins are supported as well.

Customize the uncustomizable

Fully themeable UI to fit your mood and taste. Create and share your own themes for Meadowlark!

Built with Love

Meadowlark is built by a passionate team of musicians who want better and accessible tools for everyone.
